## Key Competitive Insights

### Why LinkedIn Won't Serve Students
- LinkedIn's revenue comes from recruiters and enterprise ($17.8B in 2025)
- Students don't generate revenue until they're professionals
- LinkedIn's UI/UX is designed for professional self-promotion, not learning
- Students report feeling "imposter syndrome" on LinkedIn
- LinkedIn has no study tools, no roadmap features, no student-specific anything

### Why Google Won't Combine Their Tools
- NotebookLM and Career Dreamer are separate teams/products
- Google has repeatedly failed at social (Google+, Buzz, Wave, Orkut)
- Google builds tools for everyone; you build a platform for students specifically
- Google is US-first; India is not a priority market for these tools yet

### Why roadmap.sh Isn't Your Competitor
- roadmap.sh is a static content site with community-contributed roadmaps
- No personalization
- No social features
- Only covers developer roles
- No AI, no study tools, no opportunities
- They won't add these features — it's not their business model (ad revenue)

### Your Moat (In Order of Strength)
1. **Network effects from student data** — Every student's journey improves roadmaps for the next student
2. **Content moat** — AI-generated study materials, cached and shared, create a growing library
3. **Community moat** — Cohorts, mentorship relationships, campus communities
4. **India-first advantage** — Built for Indian student context, Hindi/regional language support potential
5. **Integrated platform** — No one else combines all 5 pillars
